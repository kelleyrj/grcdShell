Type 1 ManagerChristie Gettler

more...
Managing Type I Diabetes is important, but life can get in the way. Type I Manager can help to make treatment safer, easier, and more effective.
close

Managing Type I Diabetes can be a lot of work - remembering to test blood glucose 4-10 times a day, counting every carb and mintue of exercise, calculating and administering insulin doses - it can be overwhelming. Type I Manager is a mobile application for individuals with Type I where they can set personal reminders to test their blood glucose, change their infusion sets, and reorder supplies, help them calculate proper insulin doses, and track patterns in their blood glucose, insulin, and activity levels. The app also has a safety feature that will notify an emergency contact with a text message and the individuals GPS location should they experience a diabetic crisis.
