How It Works




3 Ways to Donate

• Sponsorship:
Contribute a monthly amount of money to a child of your choice and build a relationship with him/her.

• One Time Donation:
Contribute a one time amount of money towards any special need a child may have without making a monthly commitment.

• General Donation:
Contribute towards a maintenance, repair or construction project to better the facilities of the home.


Information Provided

• Description of Child or Project:
Provided in each donation section are excerpts of information about who or what you are contributing to.

• List of Costs:
Regardless of what you choose to donate towards, we provide a detailed list of how the money will be used.

Payment Methods

• Credit Card:
For the convenience of donors, you can safely use your credit card to donate to the home.

• Paypal:
If you do not feel comfortable paying through your credit card, we accept payments from Paypal.

• Money Transfer:
To directly transfer money to the home, money transfers are available via MoneyGram and Western Union.
