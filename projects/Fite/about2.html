Meet the Parents




The Family
The Perez family is made up of Fernando (Father), Yadira (Mother), Fernanda (Daughter) and Jose Fernando (Son).  

The Calling
A little over a decade ago, Fernando and Yadira Perez decided to help in the ministry of the Casa Hogar Mamá Paulita. What at first was supposed to be a support position quickly turned into so much more. When they had arrived to the home, Fernando was told that he was the new director of the home putting him in charge of everything. This confused and overwhelmed Fernando at first since he and his wife had come to only help but soon realized that this was their true calling from God. The Perez family has not only directed this ministry but have made it into something truly inspiring and helpful for the children who end up at their doorstep.

11 Years Strong
For the past 11 years, the Perez family turned what was a normal children's home, into a place where needy children are given the oportunity for something better in life. They have established a Christ-centered environment and help teach these children the basic life skills to have success in the future. Through the continued guidance of Fernando and Yadira, the Casa Hogar Mamá Paulita has truly flourished.
