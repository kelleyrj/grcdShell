Accountability




Where the Money Goes

• Donation Made:
The process is started by a donor who decides to contribute money towards a child or project.

• Governing Board:
After the donation is made, the money is received by the governing board in charge of the home.

• Casa Hogar Mamá Paulita:
The governing board then directs the appropriate funds to the children's home.


Feedback

• E-mail Receipt:
The moment any transaction goes through, the donor will receive an e-mail receipt for their own records.

• Donation Proof:
In the appropriate case, a photograph of the child with the donation received will be send to the donor.

• Year-end Report:
A financial report will be sent out at the end of each year to donors for tax deduction purposes.
