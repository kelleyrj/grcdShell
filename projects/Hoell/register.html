OnTour

1. Register Your Band
OnTour is a web application that helps bands plan tours. Begin by filling out the registration information. This information will be given to bands and venues in the cities you would like to tour. The more thorough you are, the better your odds at booking great gigs!

2. Plan Your Tour
Once your band information is registered, you will plan your trip! Once a route is selected, you can start selecting bands and venues to contact for booking.
