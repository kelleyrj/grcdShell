getting started is easy

all it takes is 4 simple steps

1

sign up your group

Groups can be large or small but usually 7–10 people are sent to each individual worksite

Groups leader can sign their groups up using the online registration tool. Groups may register as late as four weeks away from a trip.

Groups can be as large or as small as you would like, but typically 7-10 volunteers are sent to each worksite.

Simply choose the week you want to come and then select the number of days within that week you are able to serve. Weekend trips are available.

Register your group Fees and Due Dates

2

plan for your trip

While on an ASP trip, your group will spend much of the time getting to know the family you are serving so you need to be prepared for a truly cross-cultural experience.

We provide cultural sensitivity training materials and devotional materials so that your group may prepare for what will be a transformational experience for everyone. From what to bring, to what to expect, you will find the resources you need as a group leader to plan a successful and meaningful experience for your group.
